Our History

Centre for Promotion of Appropriate Technology (CEPAT) Was registered on 9th May 2017 as South Sudanese non-governmental organization, With Registration No. 538.

The mission of the organization is to promote, through enterprise-led approach, use of appropriate technology for implementation of sustainable grassroots projects that help provide solutions to health-related ailments, socio-economic inequalities and environmental issues by building skills, influencing people and supporting livelihoods and sustainable growth in economic opportunities.

Work
